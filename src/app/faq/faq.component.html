FAQ's

Why is MTM needed?

There are approximately 1.5 million preventable adverse events yearly due to medication mismanagement.

Who can benefit from MTM?

Anyone who is taking prescription medications, over-the-counter medications, vitamins, and supplements. Particularly those who are taking several medications, who have chronic health conditions, or those who require frequent monitoring.

What will be done at the first MTM visit?

We will start by gathering a comprehensive medication list for you and getting to know you. After gathering information, we will review medications to see if any changes need to be made and address any of your problems or concerns.

How will MTM sve me money?

Pharmacists will review your medications to ensure your medications are appropriate and the best option for you. The pharmacist can also look to see if there are cheaper alternatives or medication coupons.

Where is medication therapy management provided?

The pharmacist will set up a meeting with you, and this can be done in the convenience of your own home.

Will I have to change doctors or pharmacies?

No, you will be able to keep the same physicians and pharmacies. We will be able to contact your physician(s) and be a mediator for better communication between healthcare providers to ensure optimal patient care.
