What is MTM?

What can we do that is of value to the patient/family/provider?

Medication Therapy Management (MTM) is a service that optimizes therapeutic outcomes for patients.

We will work in collaboration with the patient, patient's family, patient's physician and other healthcare providers to identify and resolve medication-related problems.

Our goal is to provide patients with knowledge of their medications and address problems or barriers to adherence so they can gain the best possible outcome from their prescription medications, over-the-counter medications, vitamins, and supplements. We hope to reduce inappropriate medication use and hospitilizations while improving daily living.

MTM services allow patients to play an active role in their health and gives them a greater autonomy and understanding of their own health. Our partnership can increase patient compliance and provide the best treatment outcomes for each patient.
