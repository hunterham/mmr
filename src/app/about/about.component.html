About Me

Hello, thank you for visiting Med Management Resolution. My name is Rebecca Pischke, Pharm D. I graduated from Creighton University in 2001. I am a licensed pharmacist, and I am certified in Medication Therapy Management.

I started this company shortly after losing my mother. She fought a good fight against pulmonary arterial hypertension with right heart failure for about 7 months. During that time, I was working full time at a very busy pharmacy. With very little free time to evaluate my mom’s symptoms, reach out to doctors, and try to research the trial medications prescribed, I was overwhelmed and felt stressed.

Many of mom’s friends called with much appreciated condolences, and continued the conversation with medication questions. Sometimes I would spend more than an hour discussing side effects of current medications, other medication options and general information regarding disease state management. Towards the end of the conversation, they would state that “I should start a pharmacy consulting business.” So I did.

I am passionate about helping people find answers to their healthcare questions. I will work closely with you individually, so I can get to know your true goals in therapy. I will ensure that you have a clear understanding of your medication therapy and will act as your liaison for all of your providers, caregivers and pharmacies.

I look forward to helping you untangle the web of your health care and assisting you in making your own informed decisions.
